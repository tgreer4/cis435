InnerView lets students get an inner view of their interviews

How to Use InnerView?

As an unverfied student user, they are able to search or scroll through companies they are interested in. At each company's page is a thread of reviews about them and the work environment from previous student employees. As a verified student, users can create, add, edit, or delete threads that belong to them. They are also abble to add comments underneath other students' reviews.

About InnerView

InnerView allows users who are students to search through reviews of companies they are interested in. These reviews are from pevious student employees and includes the following information:
  • job title
  • the range of pay
  • tasks they had
  • company environment
